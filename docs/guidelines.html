Community Guidelines

Last updated: January 2026

Purl is a calm, encouraging space for crafters. Whether you're sharing your first wonky scarf or your hundredth masterpiece, you belong here. These guidelines exist to keep that feeling alive for everyone.

Be kind

Crafting is personal. Someone's first project might look messy to an experienced eye, but it represents real effort and pride. Treat every project, question, and post with respect.

If you don't have something supportive to say, you can keep scrolling.

Be honest

Share your real work and your real progress — wonky stitches, dropped rows, frogged projects and all. Crafting communities thrive on authenticity, not perfection.

Don't take credit for someone else's pattern, photo, or project.

Respect intellectual property

When you share a pattern, it should be your own, or one you have permission to share. Don't copy patterns from copyrighted sources (books, paid patterns, other apps) into Purl without explicit permission from the designer.

When inspired by someone else's work, credit them.

What's not allowed

The following will result in content removal and may lead to account suspension:

  • Harassment, hate speech, or threats directed at any individual or group
  • Sexually explicit content or nudity
  • Graphic violence or self-harm content
  • Spam, scams, or unrelated promotions
  • Impersonation of other users, brands, or designers
  • Copyright infringement — sharing copyrighted patterns or photos without permission
  • Doxxing or sharing private information about others
  • Illegal activity or content promoting it

How to report something

If you see something that violates these guidelines:

  • Tap the three-dot menu on the post
  • Choose "Report this post"
  • Confirm the report

Reports are reviewed within 24 hours. The user being reported is not told who reported them.

How to block someone

If another user makes you uncomfortable, you can block them:

  • Tap the three-dot menu on their post
  • Choose "Block this user"

You won't see their posts or comments anymore. They aren't notified that you blocked them. You can manage blocked users in Settings → Blocked Users.

Consequences

Depending on the severity and pattern of violations, consequences include:

  • Content removal
  • A warning
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account ban

Severe violations (such as harassment, doxxing, or illegal content) can result in immediate permanent banning.
