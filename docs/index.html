Purl
Crochet · Knitting · Sewing

A calm space for makers.

A mobile app for the crochet, knitting, and sewing community. Patterns, project tracking, and tools — built for crafters who care about their craft.

What's inside

Pattern Library

A curated library of patterns across crochet, knitting, and sewing — with sizing tools that adapt to your gauge.

Project Journal

Track your projects from cast-on to finishing, with progress, notes, and photos for each one.

Maker's Tools

Gauge calculator, yarn estimator, row counter, and stash tracker — all in one place.

Community

A social feed for makers, joinable craft clubs, and a space to share your work in progress.

About

Purl is built by an independent developer for the crafting community. It's designed to feel calm and intentional — a digital companion for the slow, satisfying work of making things by hand.

The app is currently in beta and launches on iOS in early 2026.
