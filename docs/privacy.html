Purl

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This privacy policy describes how Purl ("we," "us," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. By using Purl, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you create an account or use Purl's features, you provide:

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Username (chosen at signup)
  • Password (encrypted, never visible to us)
  • Profile information you choose to share (bio, craft preferences, skill level)
  • Content you create: pattern posts, project notes, photos, comments, and messages in clubs

Information collected automatically

When you use the app, we automatically collect:

  • Basic usage data (which features you use, how often)
  • Device information (operating system version, device type)
  • Crash and performance data to help us fix issues

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve Purl's core features (patterns, projects, community feed)
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure
  • Personalize your experience based on your craft preferences
  • Communicate important account or service updates
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent abuse, fraud, or violations of our Terms

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not show third-party advertisements in the app.

3. How Information Is Shared

Public content

Posts you make in the community feed, patterns you share to the library, and your username and profile are visible to other Purl users. Don't share anything in these areas that you wouldn't want other users to see.

Service providers

Purl uses Google Firebase (authentication, database, file storage) to operate. Firebase is bound by Google's privacy policies and processes your data on our behalf. We do not share data with any other third-party services beyond what's necessary to run the app.

Legal compliance

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Purl, our users, or the public.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

  • Passwords are encrypted using Firebase Authentication's secure hashing
  • Access to your data is controlled by Firestore security rules that prevent unauthorized reads and writes
  • All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)

No system is perfectly secure. You're responsible for keeping your password confidential and for any activity on your account.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and edit your data

You can view and edit your profile, posts, projects, and saved items at any time within the app.

Delete your account

You can delete your account from Settings → Delete my account. This permanently removes your posts, profile, and personal data from our systems. Some content (such as comments others have responded to) may remain in anonymized form.

Block and report

If another user is posting abusive content or harassing you, you can block them or report their post from the post's menu. Reports are reviewed by our moderation team.

6. Children's Privacy

Purl is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us at the email below and we will delete the account.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify users through the app or by email. Continued use of Purl after changes means you accept the updated policy.
